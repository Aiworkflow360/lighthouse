What is Lighthouse?

Lighthouse is a free web app that creates personalised action plans for parents and carers of children with disabilities or additional needs. Within 60 seconds of entering a diagnosis, families receive a clear, prioritised list of next steps, local services, financial support, and emotional resources.

Who is it for?

Parents, carers, and family members of children who have received a diagnosis of a disability, developmental condition, or additional need. Particularly valuable at the point of diagnosis when families feel most overwhelmed and unsure where to turn.

What it provides
  • Personalised, prioritised action plans tailored to each condition
  • Local service finder with verified organisations
  • Letter templates for schools, GPs, and local authorities
  • Financial support guidance (DLA, Carer's Allowance, grants)
  • Emotional support signposting and peer networks
  • EHCP and education rights information
Privacy & Data
  • No personal data collected whatsoever
  • No cookies, no tracking, no analytics
  • No account creation or signup required
  • Fully open source — code is publicly auditable
  • No commercial interests or advertising

Safe to recommend with confidence — families share zero personal information.

How to recommend to families

Hand this information to families after diagnosis appointments, or mention it during consultations. Families can scan the QR code below or visit the website directly. No technical knowledge is required — the guided experience takes under 60 seconds on any smartphone.

“There’s a free tool called Lighthouse that can help you with next steps. Just scan this QR code with your phone — it’ll show you exactly what to do.”
